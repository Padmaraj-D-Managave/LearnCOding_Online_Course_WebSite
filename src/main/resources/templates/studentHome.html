Who is Student/Trainee?

A trainee is an individual who is undergoing a period of learning, development, and practical experience in a specific field or occupation. Traineeships are designed to provide hands-on training, allowing individuals to acquire new skills, knowledge, and competencies in preparation for their future careers.

Traineeships offer a valuable opportunity for individuals to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. During this period, trainees work under the guidance and supervision of experienced professionals, who provide mentorship and support to help them develop their abilities and gain practical experience.
